Soccer as a hobby

Soccer is not just a hobby and a sport for me, it's also a way for me to "run away" from reality. It's a way for me to have fun, meet new people, and really be in the moment.

Soccer arent just for the practice and staying active, it's also a good way for your brain to relax. It's always been something I've been good at, and that's very motivating for me.

Benefits of playing soccer

  • Improve fitness and strength
  • Builds teamwork skills
  • Builds new friendships
  • Build community
  • Reduce stress
  • Teaches discipline and responsebility
  • Good for mental health and phusical health
  • Get better sleep
